Modular Services

Unique One Network Multichain Marketplace

Unique One Network transforms our collection of dApp Marketplaces on popular EVM blockchains into a full-service Multichain NFT Hub with a Hub and Spoke Model.

The Unique One Network Multichain NFT Marketplace not only supports Mega Auctions with multiple auction mechanisms but also philanthropic and charity fundraising through Unique One Love — our philanthropic crowdfunding venture.

Marketplace Launchpad

Unique One Network can deploy NFT marketplaces with out-of-the box theme options, infrastructure, and support.

Many teams, projects, and companies desire NFT marketplaces to compliment their product and marketing strategy. But bootstrapping an NFT marketplace is time-consuming, laborious, and capital intensive. Now you can launch an NFT Marketplace without financial risk by staking $UNET tokens.

Choose from a variety of themes with a set of NFT related API and SDK tools for the theme framework of the NFT marketplace. Or level up for customized premium themes and features.

UniqueOne.World Metaverse Land Launchport

Unique One offers a novel means for individuals and projects to secure metaverse lands in a flexible and scalable manner via an exciting and innovative staking mechanism for metaverse land leases.

By partnership and collaboration with Realitychain.io, users can acquire metaverse lands for free by staking $UNET tokens. Multiple lease terms provide the flexibility to build for fun, long-term projects, or even temporary events.

Initial Platform Launchpad Offering (IPLO)

IPLO is our proprietary service offered to add marketing, community, and auction support to teams who wish to release their utility & governance tokens to their community via NFT sales or auction events on the Unique One Network NFT Marketplace.

Projects that choose to hold IPLOs on the Unique One Network Marketplace gain exposure to Unique One Network Communities, Octopus Network Communities, and NEAR Protocol Meta-Communities.

Staking $UNET

All of our services will be supported by our native token $UNET.

$UNET collateral staking mechanisms unlock a variety of Modular Services on the Unique One Network as well as offer opportunities for retail investors and protocol launches to spin up farming pools.

Validators and Delegators who stake $OCT tokens on the Unique One Network appchain in the Octopus Network receive $UNET tokens as staking rewards.
