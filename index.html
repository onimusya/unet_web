Initial Platform Launchpad Offering (IPLO)

IPLO is a proprietary service to add marketing, community, and auction support to teams who wish to release their native tokens to their community via unlockable avenues attached to NFT sales or auction events.

IPLOs are popular crowdfunding events that can be customized to each individual protocol depending on their goals.

Projects that choose to hold IPLOs on the Unique One Network Marketplace gain the advantage of the network effects of the UON Marketing funnel to the entire Unique One Network, Octopus Network, and NEAR Protocol Meta-Communities.
